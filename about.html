Our Story

Born in Pune, Serving All of India

SFV Sales and Distribution Pvt. Ltd. was founded with a single, powerful mission: to bridge the gap between aspiring homeowners and affordable home financing across India.

Headquartered in Pune, Maharashtra, we recognized that millions of Indians dream of owning their home but struggle with complex loan processes, high interest rates, and opaque banking systems.

We built SFV Loans to be radically different — faster approvals, transparent processes, competitive rates, and a dedicated relationship manager for every customer, regardless of loan size.

Today, we proudly serve customers across 28 states with a growing portfolio of ₹500 Crore+ in sanctioned loans.
